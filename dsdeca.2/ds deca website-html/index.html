What is DECA?

"DECA prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management."

Finance & Accounting Use financial analysis and accounting to solve problems, create budgets, and show expertise. Learn more
Business Management & Administration Create business plans emphasizing leadership, strategy, and management skills. Learn more
Hospitality & Tourism Manage events, develop campaigns, and tackle challenges in hospitality and tourism. Learn more
